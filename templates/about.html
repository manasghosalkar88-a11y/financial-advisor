About Smart Financial Advisor

Mini banking analytics project – perfect for JP Morgan / Deutsche Bank interviews.

This tool estimates EMI using a standard bank formula, predicts loan approval probability using a machine learning model, and gives a simple risk and credit-score based advisory. It also supports optional prepayment analysis, visual charts, and downloadable PDF/CSV reports.

  • EMI calculator with interest & principal breakdown
  • AI-based approval probability (classification model)
  • Risk score using EMI/Income ratio and credit score
  • Prepayment impact – interest saved & tenure reduced
  • Exportable PDF EMI report and full CSV amortization schedule
  • Built using Flask, Python, HTML/CSS, and Chart.js
